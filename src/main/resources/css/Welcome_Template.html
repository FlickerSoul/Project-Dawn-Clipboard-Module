  • If you want this dictionary always stay on top, please toggle the first switch.
  • You can copy anything you want, and they will be automatically searched in this dictionary.
  • You can also search anything you want in the top-right search bar
  • If you want to turn of the listening clipboard function, please click the second switch on the left-top cornor.
  • This application plays and caches pronunciation audio automatically. If you want to turn of the auto-pronunciation, please turn of the third switch.
  • If you use Kindle PC edtion and want to search words in Kindle, please toggle the forth switch and just copy the words you want to look up.
More Uses
    Shortcuts:
  • CTRL+SHIFT+E/C/S: switch among four tabs
  • CTRL+SHIFT+S: switch to search tab and automatically search current input in Google
  • CTRL+Alt+X: minimize, maximize, or focus on the application window
  • ESC: in-app minimize application window
  • CTRL+SHIFT+F: search bar input focus
  • CTRL+Q: pronunciate the displayed word
  • Alt+←/→: switch between words in the search history
    Tips
  • Left Click On Hyperlike to search the Hyperred word
  • Right Click On Selected Word In application to search the selected word
